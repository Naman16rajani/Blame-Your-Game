Why PARENT PAGE?

This is a serious thing to discuss as nobody can understand their kids as their parents. We can guide these parents in the right direction for their kids. we hope that it’s helpful...

SERIOUS ALERT CHILDREN THESE DAYS GO THROUGH SOME SERIOUS MENTAL HEALTH ISSUES INCLUDING ANXIETY, DEPRESSION, PANIC ATTACKS ETC. WHICH NEEDS TO BE LOOKED UPON AND GIVE ATTENTION TO. SO WE ARE HERE TO HELP THEM AND PROVIDE THEM AS MUCH AS WE CAN.

We are providing helping guidelines and some tips over here to make it better:

1. Try to listen to your kid rather than telling him/her what to do.
2. Be kind and know what they’re actually going through.
3. Let them know that THEY ARE IMPORTANT AND VALUED.
4. Make them go to a counselor/psychiatrist.
5. THERE IS NO SHAME IN GOING TO A COUNSELOR/PSYCHIATRIST.
6. Give them space and don’t give them attention only because you know they need it because OVERTHINKING IS WHAT THEY MIGHT BE DOING.
7. HELP HELP HELP AND LISTEN.
